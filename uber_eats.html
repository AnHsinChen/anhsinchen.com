UX Case Study of Uber Eats App

A UX Analysis and AI-Powered Solution for Uber Eats.

OVERVIEW

The aim of this project was to identify and solve UX issues in food delivery apps through AI-powered solutions.

As an independent UX researcher and designer, I conducted a analysis of Uber Eats' mobile experience applying Benchmark UX Performances guideline developed by Baymard. This case study uncovered 34 usability issues across 4 key user flows, and designed AI solutions that transform how users discover and order food, ultimately proposing intelligent features that could improve user satisfaction and business outcomes.

ROLE & DURATION

UX Researcher & Product Designer
UX/UI Design, App Usability Research, AI Product Strategy & Prototyping

1 Week

Set Objective

Before starting any analysis, I first defined the main user flows that I wanted to examine:

  • Restaurant Discovery (Browse, search, filter restaurants)
  • Menu Navigation & Item Selection (View menu, customize items, add to cart)
  • Checkout & Payment (Review order, apply promos, complete payment)

Method

I used Baymard Institute's UX guidelines as a comprehensive checklist for identifying usability issues. I created a custom evaluation framework that documented each issue with severity ratings, frequency estimates, and priority scores, allowing me to systematically assess user flows and prioritize the most critical problems.

Fig: Issue Tracking System Framework Overview

I also looked at DoorDash, the strongest competitor to Uber Eats in the North American online food delivery market. In fact, DoorDash has a larger market share than Uber in the US. I tried the main user flows on both apps and documented key observations.

Table Results and Insights

I identified 34 issues across 4 main user flows. Among them, there are 11 issues with Priority Scores (severity Ã— frequency) above 8. I prioritized them and wrote UX suggestions for each. I then consolidated and curated these into 6 insights.

Fig: Uber East Mobile App UX Analysis Table Results

Here are 3 noticable insights:

  • Search & Discovery is Fundamentally Broken: 3 out of 11 high-priority issues are search-related, including the highest priority issue (UE-S3: Priority 12). The core problem is that users cannot efficiently find what they want to eat, forcing them into repetitive ordering patterns that limit both user satisfaction and business growth. This poor discovery experience directly constrains order variety and reduces restaurant exposure, creating a lose-lose scenario for both users and restaurant partners.
  • Multi-Service Integration Creates UX Confusion: Three high-priority issues stem from Uber's attempt to integrate ride and food services (UE-AC2, UE-AC3, UE-AC5). This creates a strategic tension between business efficiency and user clarity, where saved addresses work seamlessly for rides but fail for food delivery, creating frustrating expectation mismatches.
  • Competitive Disadvantages Hidden in Plain Sight: Despite promotions being Uber's key value proposition, they're buried in the interface (UE-CG4: Priority 12). This ironic failure undermines a strong business advantage through poor UX design, creating market risk where users may perceive Uber as more expensive than competitors like DoorDash.

AI Application for Uber Eats

This AI solution transforms food discovery from frustrating keyword searches into intelligent conversations that actually understand what users want.
Through personalized taste profiling, custom filtering, and smart recommendations, the design tackles both the broken search experience and decision paralysis that plague current food apps.

Conversational Search Engine

Ask natural questions like "What's the best ramen near me?" and get intelligent, cross-restaurant comparisons with personalized recommendations.

Custom Filter Creation

AI learns your preferences to create personalized tags and filters that automatically surface relevant food while hiding incompatible options.

AI-Powered Taste Matching

Personalized compatibility scores and explanations for every dish help users make confident decisions with insights like "Perfect for your spice tolerance and healthy eating goals.
