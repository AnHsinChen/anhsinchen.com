Care Anywhere

Modular equipment design for home-based dental care

OVERVIEW

En Chu Kong Hospital in New Taipei City operates one of Taiwan's pioneering in-home dental care programs, providing essential oral health services to bedridden and homebound patients who cannot access traditional dental clinics.

This personal project explores how thoughtful equipment design can transform in-home dental care from a logistical burden into an efficient practice.

By developing a modular system that streamlines setup and adapts to any home environment, I aimed to make mobile dental care more accessible that encourages more dental professionals to participate in home care services, ultimately improving oral healthcare access for Taiwan's most vulnerable populations.

Research Synthesis

I conducted a field study by accompanying an in-home dental team on visits to bedridden patients. With consent, I recorded video, observed their workflow, and interviewed the dentists to better understand their pain points. I then transcribed and coded the interviews and observations, synthesizing the findings into three key insights.

Insights

Kicking off the Design

Unlike the static environment of a dental clinic, in-home treatments demand solutions that are mobile, efficient, and flexible. Inspired by these unique challenges, I explored design possibilities by looking at products outside of dentistry that embody adaptability. This exploration led me to the idea of modular design as a starting point. Through sketching, I began envisioning how the three core devices dentists rely on could merge into a single, integrated solution.

Integrated Cable Routing

Each module includes dedicated grooves that keep cables neatly organized. This reduces tangling risks during transport and ensures a faster, smoother setup process.

Independent Operation

The unified interface allows each module to function as a standalone unit when needed, giving dental teams flexibility to adapt to different treatment scenarios.

Enhanced Stability Mechanism

A secure mounting system keeps modules firmly in place during transport while allowing quick detachment for efficient setup and teardown.
